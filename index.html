We are Beat 'Em Hub

We are a Berlin-based Gaming Network who specializes in creating immersive gaming events in order to give a space for both gamers and non-gamers alike, including professionals within the gaming industry to play, connect and foster creativity.

We aim to create a safe space to create and grow communities in all aspects of gaming.

Our team has experience around many aspects & services such as gaming, event organization, IT, light & illumination, DJing, sound & visual design, performance art, project handling, client management, situation analysis, marketing strategy, creative concept, copywriting, content, journalism, Social Media, photography, film, drones, gimbals, controlling, on- & offline.

Services

We try to cover most aspects of the gaming world from Retro & Moddern Gaming, Indie Gaming, Virtual Reality, Lan Parties, Card Games (e.g. Magic the Gathering), Pen & Paper (e.g. Dungeons & Dragons), Boardgames, Esports, Tournaments.

For events, we enable a variety of gaming, indoor areas (e.g. bars, clubs), & outdoor areas (e.g. festivals, food fairs), Branded Events (e.g. our arcade machines with customized branding), Live Music, Streaming Services, Event Moderation, On-Site Maintenance & Support, Content Production

Gaming Areas can be implemented almost anywhere! From bars, clubs, festivals, corporate events, schools, community & cultural events and more!
